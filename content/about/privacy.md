# Privacy Policy

## 1. Overview

System Resource Hub is designed to be a simple, privacy-respecting resource. This Privacy Policy explains what data is (and isn’t) collected when you use the site.

The goal is to provide information, not to collect it.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data, limited to:

- **Anonymous analytics data** (via Umami), such as page views and general device/browser information
- **Information you voluntarily provide**, such as emails, messages, or submitted resources

We do **not** collect personally identifiable information through tracking, accounts, or hidden data collection.

---

## 3. Analytics

This site uses privacy-friendly analytics (Umami) to understand general usage, such as:

- Page views
- Referring sites
- Basic device or browser information

This data is:

- Not personally identifiable
- Not used to track individual users
- Not shared or sold

No cookies are used for analytics.

---

## 4. How Information Is Used

Any information collected is used only to:

- Maintain and improve the site
- Review submitted tools or resources
- Respond to messages or inquiries

We do not build user profiles or track behavior across websites.

---

## 5. Voluntary Submissions

If you choose to contact or contribute (for example, via email or submission forms), the information you provide may include:

- Email address
- Message content
- Submitted tool or resource details

This information is used only for communication or review purposes and is never sold or shared.

---

## 6. External Links

This site links to third-party tools and services.

System Resource Hub is not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites. You are encouraged to review their policies separately.

---

## 7. Data Practices

System Resource Hub does **not**:

- Track users across the web
- Build personal profiles
- Use invasive tracking methods
- Sell or share user data

---

## 8. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect any information you choose to provide. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

---

## 9. Your Choices

You are not required to provide any personal information to use this site.

If you contact the site and would like your message or information removed, you can request that via email.

---

## 10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach out:

- Email: systemresourcehub@gmail.com

---

## 11. Updates

This policy may be updated if the site changes in the future. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
