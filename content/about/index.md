# About System Resource Hub

System Resource Hub exists for a pretty simple reason:

**finding tools for plural systems shouldn’t be this hard.**

Most of what’s out there is scattered, buried, or only shared inside specific spaces. And even when you *do* find something, it’s hard to tell if it’ll actually work for you.

This site is meant to make that easier.

---

## What This Is

- A directory of tools for plural systems
- A way to find alternatives without digging for hours
- A place that tries to keep things clear and actually usable

It’s not trying to list *everything*. Just things that are worth knowing about.

---

## Who This Is For

- Systems trying to find tools that actually fit
- People questioning or figuring things out
- Anyone supporting a system and looking for resources

---

## Why Alternatives Matter

No tool works for everyone.
Something that’s perfect for one system might be unusable for another. Needs, preferences, energy levels vary from one person to the next.
So instead of pushing “the best” option, this exists so you can see what’s out there and decide for yourself.

---

## Approach

This site doesn’t filter tools based on morals, drama, or how something was made.
You might care about those things. You might not.
Either way, that choice should be yours, not decided for you ahead of time.
The goal here is just to give you the information in a way that’s actually usable.

For more on how this site approaches inclusion, safety, and resource selection, see the [Philosophy](/philosophy) page.

---

## How to Use This Site

- Browse tools based on what you need
- Look at alternatives if something isn’t working
- Use energy levels if you’re low and need something easier

---

## How It Grows

This isn’t a finished project.
Things get added, changed, or removed over time. Tools disappear. New ones show up. Stuff improves.
A lot of what ends up here comes from people submitting things or pointing out gaps.
