# Philosophy

## Overview

System Resource Hub exists to make finding tools easier.

But more than that, it exists to do it in a way that **respects the people using it**.

This space is complicated. Needs vary. Experiences vary. What helps one system might not help another.

This site is built with that in mind.

---

## Core Values

### Usefulness over Noise

There’s a lot of information out there, but not all of it is usable.

This site focuses on tools that people can realistically use, not just what’s popular, aesthetic, or widely shared.

The goal is to reduce friction, not add to it.

---

### Choice over Curation Bias

This site does not decide what is “best” for you.

Instead of pushing a single option, it shows multiple tools and alternatives so you can decide what fits your needs, energy, and preferences.

Different systems function differently. That matters.

---

### Accessibility Matters

Not everyone has the same:

- Energy levels
- Cognitive load capacity
- Time
- Experience

Tools are not one-size-fits-all, and neither is the way people search for them.

This site tries to make discovery easier, especially when energy is low or overwhelm is high.

---

### Non-gatekeeping

This space includes people with different experiences, frameworks, and ways of understanding themselves.

This site does not attempt to define who “counts” or who should or shouldn’t be using these resources.

If something is useful to you, that’s what matters.

---

### Neutral, not Careless

This site does not filter tools based on discourse, personal opinions, or community drama.

However, neutrality does not mean ignoring harm.

If something is clearly unsafe, exploitative, or misleading, it does not belong here.

The goal is to provide options, not to platform harm.

---

## How Resources Are Selected

Tools listed here are chosen based on things like:

- Practical usefulness
- Clarity of purpose
- Accessibility and ease of use
- Relevance to plural systems

This is not an exhaustive directory.

It is a **curated collection of tools worth knowing about**.

---

## What This Site Is Not

System Resource Hub is not:

- A medical or mental health service
- A diagnostic tool
- A replacement for professional care
- A social platform

It does not provide treatment, advice, or crisis support.

---

## External Tools

Most tools listed here are created and maintained by third parties.

This means:

- Their features can change
- Their privacy practices may differ
- Their safety and moderation vary

You are encouraged to use your own judgment and review their policies when needed.

---

## Responsibility and Autonomy

This site is designed to give you information, not make decisions for you.

What you choose to use, trust, or engage with is up to you.

That autonomy is intentional.

---

## Closing

This is a small, evolving project.

It is built to be:

- Useful
- Respectful
- Accessible

Not perfect.

If it helps you find something that works, even once, then it’s doing what it was made to do.
