## Simply Plural is Shutting down

### TL;DR

Simply Plural is being discontinued. The servers will stay up until at least June 1, 2026, but they will shut down after that. You should start planning to move to another app.

---

## What’s Happening

Simply Plural’s developer has officially announced that development is stopping, and the app will eventually lose server support.

The app will still be available for download, and the servers will stay online for now. The current estimate is that they will remain up until at least June 1, 2026, with a 1–2 month warning before shutdown.

After the servers go down:

- The app will only work in offline mode
- Some features will stop working entirely
- You won’t be able to log in on a new device

---

## What This means for You

If you’re using Simply Plural right now, this is the important part.

- Offline mode is not designed for long-term use and may break or behave inconsistently over time
- Features that rely on servers, like friends and syncing, will stop working completely
- Your data will *only* be saved locally per device once the servers go dark. 

This isn’t something you need to panic about today, but it is something you should start preparing for.

---

## What You Should Do next

You don’t need to rush, but don’t ignore it either.

Start with this:

- Export your data from Simply Plural
- Look into alternative apps and see what fits your needs
- Test other tools before you fully switch

If you wait until the last minute, you’re going to be doing all of this under pressure. It’s a lot easier to do it gradually.
