## Submission Guidelines

If you’re submitting something to be listed here, keep it simple.

This site is curated. Not everything gets in.

---

## What You Can Submit

- Tools, apps, templates, or guides that are actually useful
- Resources people can *use*, not just look at
- Anything you have permission to share

If it doesn’t help someone in a real way, it’s not a fit.

---

## Requirements

Your submission needs to:

- Be readable and accessible (skip the aesthetic font spam)
- Respect user privacy
- Clearly show what’s free vs paid
- Actually work

If someone has to fight your setup to use it, it’s getting skipped.

---

## What Doesn’t Fit Here

- Conflict-driven content, callouts, or drama-adjacent anything
- Tools that break platform rules or put users at risk
- Misleading setups (hidden costs, vague access, bait descriptions)
- Personal or identifying info left in submissions

If it creates friction, confusion, or problems it’s out.

---

## Notes

- Submissions may be edited, declined, or removed without notice
- This is a **curated resource hub**, not a public directory
- Paid resources are fine, just label them honestly.
